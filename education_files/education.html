PhD in Trustworthy AI  Lund University, Sweden

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Electrical and Information Technology at Lund University, Sweden, funded by the Wallenberg AI, Autonomous Systems and Software Program (WASP), and have been pursuing my doctoral studies since April 2022.
Under the supervision of Assoc. Prof. Amir Aminifar , Assoc. Prof. Ahmed Rezine , and Prof. Maria Kihl , my research centers on reliable decision-making in machine learning, with a particular focus on applications in biomedical devices and control systems. As part of this work, I have developed scalable methods for verifying deep neural networks and investigated approaches to improving model reliability in terms of robustness, fairness, and privacy.
